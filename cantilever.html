Cantilever Bridge

Cantilever bridges use structures that project horizontally into space, supported on only one end. These bridges balance weight through counterweights and are often used for medium spans.

Suitability

Medium spans where it's challenging to place supports in the middle.

Suitable for rugged terrains or water crossings.

Effective for areas with high ship traffic underneath.

Pros and Cons

Pros

Requires fewer piers.

Suitable for spans with difficult access.

Can handle heavy loads.

Cons

Complex design and construction.

High maintenance costs.

Prone to oscillations under heavy winds.
