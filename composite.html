Composite Bridge

Composite bridges combine materials like steel and concrete to maximize strength, durability, and cost-efficiency.

Suitability

Areas requiring high strength-to-weight ratio.

Suitable for both short and long spans.

Effective for highways and railroads.

Pros and Cons

Pros

Combines the benefits of materials.

Durable and efficient for heavy traffic.

Flexible design possibilities.

Cons

Higher initial cost.

Maintenance can be complex.
